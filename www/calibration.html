Tips and Tricks

Too hard?

If you're having trouble, try:
  • put a bean bag/stuffed animal on your head to prevent head movement
  • close one eye and train until you can try two eyes again

Too easy?

If you've got the exercises down, try:
  • decreasing the target size
  • follow the target with your mouse and your eyes
  • stand on one leg or do math exercises while training
  • try standing further/closer from the screen
